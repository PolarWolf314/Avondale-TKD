OUR INSTRUCTORS

ROBERT IRELAND II DAN

Robert started his career in 1995, and has been trained by many great instructors. These include: Andrew Niven IV Dan, Hoog Looi III Dan, Master Peter Graham VII Dan, and Shaun Tolley IV Dan.

It is with a wealth of instructing styles and experiences, that enabled Robert to take over the head instructor position at Avondale Taekwon-Do School.

JOANNA LIU I DAN

Joanna is the embodiment of what it means to be an Avondale Taekwon-Do student. Joanna has trained at Avondale since 2013 and is liked by all students, young and old.

She is a role model for all students working towards black belt. As a 1st degree black belt, Joanna assists us in teaching our classes.
