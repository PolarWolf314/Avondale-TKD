WHY TRAIN WITH US?

At Avondale Taekwon-Do, we have over 20 years of experience teaching students the latest techniques, using the latest equipment.

We pride ourselves on running mixed classes for all ages, to develop a balanced curriculum that covers all aspects of the art that is ITF Taekwon-Do.

Avondale ITF Taekwon-Do is run by Robert Ireland - II Dan. His philosophy believes that everyone has something to offer in class, no matter how small, no matter your abilities. We are inclusive of everyone.

WHERE DO WE TRAIN?

We hire our venues. Currently, we are located at Avondale Primary School on Crayford Street, West, Avondale. It is on the corner of Great North Road & Crayford Street - in the middle of the Avondale Shopping Centre.

Our times are Monday: 6:30pm - 8:00pm, and Thursday: 6:30pm - 8:00pm.

TENETS OF TAEKWON-DO

예의

(Ye-ui)

Courtesy

To be polite.
To encourage justice.
To encourage humanity.
To distinguish rank.
To have etiquette.
To respect others' possessions.

염치

(Yom-chi)

Integrity

To know right from wrong.
To have a conscience.
To always try to do the right thing.
To train hard, but not for ego.

인내

(In-nae)

Perseverance

To never give up.
To always be patient.
To set and follow goals.

극기

(Guk-gi)


Self Control

To never misuse Taekwon-Do.
To have self-control, at all times.
To defeat your own flaws, rather than defeating others.

백절불골

(Baek-jul Bool-gool)

Indomitable Spirit

To persevere, even in the face of death.
To never hesitate dealing with injustice.
To be modest and honest at all times.
